User Guide

About Our System

Welcome to StayOnTrack. This website is created to predict student retention using data from the School of Engineering and Architecture. We utilize an XGBoost model to reliably forecast student retention.

How to Use Our Application

  • Upload Your Data: Upload your Excel file in .xlsx format via the "Browse" button for automated retention predictions and analysis.

  • Review Your Results: Once your data is submitted, the platform will display a detailed retention prediction with easy-to-understand outputs such as graphs, statistics, and insights.
